About Me

I'm Aung, and I'm a 2nd Year Law Student!

I graduated from the University of Southern California in May, 2021 with a BA in Philosophy. I began to attend St. John's Law School in August, 2024.

As a Juris Doctor candidate at St. John's University School of Law, I am building a legal career on a solid foundation of technology and analytics. My prior experience as a software developer, where I designed and maintained database-driven applications, has equipped me with a unique problem-solving perspective that I now apply to complex legal issues. My legal experience is centered on transactional law, including supporting real estate closings, drafting estate planning documents, and researching the evolution of financial regulation. I am passionate about the intersection of law and technology and am eager to leverage my distinctive skill set to provide innovative and efficient solutions in the legal field.
